# 3-nomic
## Current rules
`1.` Players pretend this nomic has way more players.  
`2.` Players pretend they have low trust in other players and software.  
`3.` A proposal passes with a simple majority of "Yes" votes, provided that "No" votes do not exceed 20% of the total poll participants.  
`4.` Candidates posts their programs to ⁠3-nomic-candidates.  
`5.` Edited program disqalifies program from election.  
`6.` Player is elected to execute his program if he wins a timepoll in this format  
`/timepoll question: Election time: 2d answer-1:` [program ID] `answer-2:` [program ID] ...  
`7.` Any player may register an ordered list of program IDs under a new, unique ID in #3-nomic-candidates.  
`8.` Election winner has 2 days to execute his program.    
`9.` Programs must be registered no earlier than four days and no later than two days before the election in ⁠3-nomic-candidates.
